Privacy Policy for "Naked Truth"

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed by the "Naked Truth" mobile application (the "App"), for what purposes, how long data is stored, and how users can manage their data.

1. Who we are

"Naked Truth" is a conversation card app for couples, friends, and adults.

Support and privacy contact: grafmk1523@gmail.com, +380637211192

2. What data we process

2.1 Account data (Google / Apple Sign-In, Firebase Authentication)

  • Firebase user identifier (uid)
  • Google/Apple account identity information (as provided by the auth provider)

2.2 App and social data

  • In-app profile/settings data
  • Friend code and friend connection data
  • Chat/interaction metadata used for social features (if enabled)

2.3 Location data (optional)

  • Approximate/current location only after permission is granted
  • Used for distance-related features between connected users

2.4 Purchase data

  • Purchase status and restore information from Apple/Google billing services

3. Where and how data is stored

  • On device: local app storage and preferences.
  • In cloud: Firebase services (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Storage), where applicable.

4. Why we use data

Data is used only for:

  • authentication and account management;
  • core app functionality;
  • social/friend features;
  • optional location-based features;
  • purchase verification/restoration;
  • app reliability and security.

We do not sell personal data.

5. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play billing infrastructure

These services process data under their own policies:

  • Firebase Data Processing and Privacy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

6. User control

You can manage your data in the App by changing settings, editing profile data, and removing app content where available.

7. Data retention and deletion

  • Local data remains on your device until deleted by you or app reset/removal.
  • Cloud data is retained as needed for account functionality unless deletion is requested.

To request deletion related to your account data, contact us via grafmk1523@gmail.com or +380637211192.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method is 100% secure.

9. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law).

10. Policy updates

We may update this Policy from time to time. The latest version always includes the "Last updated" date.
